## ADBE — Adobe Inc.

**Archetype:** Minimal hedger (IR swaps + FX forwards)
**Filings processed:** 5 (1 10-K ending 2025-11-28, 4 10-Qs)

### Derivative Profile
- **FX forwards:** $6.54B notional across EUR, JPY, GBP, AUD, CAD
  - Cash flow hedges on forecasted revenue/expenses (up to 24-month maturity)
  - Non-designated hedges on foreign currency monetary assets/liabilities
  - Does not hedge to entirely offset FX effects
- **IR swaps:** $2.70B notional converting fixed-rate senior notes to floating (SOFR OIS)
  - Ramped up from $1.20B in Q1 2025 to $2.70B by Q2 2025
- **Senior notes outstanding:** $6.15B (carrying $6.21B, fair value $6.18B)

### Key Sensitivities
- **FX:** 10% shift in exchange rates = $456M change in hedging instrument fair value
- **IR:** 50bp move = $71M change in fair value of hedged fixed-rate debt
- **Short-term investments:** 150bp move = $4M impact on $1.16B portfolio

### Notable Observations
- Counterparty risk mitigated via master netting and collateral security agreements
- 10-Q filings largely cross-reference the 10-K, causing extraction gaps on interim periods
- Unhedged senior notes bear fixed rates with no P&L risk from rate changes
